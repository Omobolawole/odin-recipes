Moimoi

Description

Ingredients

  • Beans
  • Palm Oil
  • Spices
  • Boiled Eggs
  • Vegetables
  • Leaves

Steps

  1. Wash and blend the beans
  2. Add the spices and palm oil
  3. Mix properly
  4. Add the boiled eggs and vegetables
  5. Scoop in leaves and wrap properly
  6. Carefully place in pot of water and boil

This is a delicious dish popularly asscociated with the Yoruba tribe in Nigeria.
