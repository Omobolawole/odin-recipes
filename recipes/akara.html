Akara

Jump to Bottom

Description

Ingredients

  • Beans
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Spices
  • Smoked Fish
  • Vegetables

Steps

  1. Wash and blend the beans
  2. Add the spices and vegetable oil
  3. Mix properly
  4. Add the smoked fish and vegetables
  5. Scoop in hot oil to fry in batches

This is a delicious dish popularly asscociated with the Yoruba tribe in Nigeria.
