Eba

Description

Ingredients

  • Garri
  • Hot Water
  • Sea-food Okra

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Pour some boiled water in a plastic bowl
  3. Gradually add garri to the bowl and cover it for a 2 minutes
  4. Mix the garri with the water until consistent
  5. Scoop in plate
  6. Serve with Sea-food Okra

This is a delicious dish popularly asscociated with the Yoruba tribe in Nigeria.
